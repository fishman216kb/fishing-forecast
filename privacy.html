Overview

216fishing.com respects your privacy. This page explains how we collect and use information when you visit our site.

Analytics (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are visited and general navigation patterns). GA4 uses cookies that help us measure site performance so we can improve features for local anglers and boaters.

Learn more about how Google uses data from partner sites here: Google Privacy & Terms.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. On 216fishing.com, cookies are used only for analytics purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some site features may be limited if cookies are blocked.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Clear or block cookies in your browser settings.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or trade personal information. Data collected via Google Analytics is aggregated and not used to identify individual users.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at admin@216fishing.com.
