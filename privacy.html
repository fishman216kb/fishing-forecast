Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Overview

216fishing.com respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit our site.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are visited and how users navigate). Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data. This helps us improve the site and provide more useful fishing information.

Google may also process information as described in their Privacy & Terms.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. On this site, they are used only for analytics purposes. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site functionality may be limited.

Your Choices

  • You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • You can clear or block cookies through your browser settings.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information. Data collected through Google Analytics is aggregated and does not identify individual users.
