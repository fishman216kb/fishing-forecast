About 216 Fishing

216 Fishing started as a Lake Erie forecast built to help plan better days on the water. What began as a simple resource for local anglers has grown into a lineup of practical, reliable trolling gear built for real Great Lakes conditions.

Our worm harnesses and accessories are designed, built, and run in Northeast Ohio. Every product we offer is tested on the water — not just made to sell, but made to fish.

The Forecast

The original forecast page is still available and continues to bring together weather forecasts, buoy data, and live camera links to help plan trips on Lake Erie near Cleveland. We reference trusted sources such as NOAA and publicly available marine data feeds.
